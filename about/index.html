About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Sheffield, where I completed my undergraduate degree in English Literature and Language and achieved a 2:1. I have a keen interest in politics, social issues and writing so I have combined those interests to pursue a number of journalism work experience opportunities. This website acts as a portfolio of the work I have written for a variety of publications, as well as articles I have written for fun to gain more experience.

I am currently working as a proofreader in Edinburgh but I am still looking for roles that would fit my experience and interests better. I am driven by the core values of justice, accountability and transparency, which is why using social platforms to speak up against injustices is so important to me. I am very active on Twitter and enjoy learning and sharing content that aims to hold people to account. However, this website I have created is especially helpful to me when speaking out against world issues and expressing my thoughts, as there are less limitations. Being able to delve into issues in depth, do thorough research, and explore counter arguments to my points is essential for me to deliver articles that I can stand by proudly.

Any support for my website is greatly appreciated and I hope you enjoy reading.
